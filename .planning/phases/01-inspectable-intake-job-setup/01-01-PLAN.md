<interfaces>
<!-- New contracts created here become the blueprints for downstream plans. -->

Required model intent:
- `TargetRequest`: user-submitted URL plus target kind when known (`auto`, `video`, `playlist`).
- `SubtitleTrack`: language code/name plus track kind (`manual` or `automatic`) and source format when available.
- `InspectItem`: normalized inspected video/playlist item containing `video_id`, title, webpage URL, and available subtitle tracks.
- `JobOptions`: CLI-configurable output root and basic inspect/job options; no complex global option system yet per D-04.
- `OutputIdentity`: stable item identity and planned bundle paths; canonical identity must include `video_id`.

Required policy intent:
- `build_output_identity(item, options)` returns a stable identity containing the YouTube video ID.
- `plan_item_paths(identity, options)` returns deterministic paths under the selected output directory.
</interfaces>
